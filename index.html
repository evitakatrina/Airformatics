What is Airformatic?

Airformatic is a real-time aerodynamic optimization tool for cyclists. It combines a camera-based posture tracker with performance sensors and an AI-powered analytics engine.

Key Features:

  • Live posture correction via visual or haptic feedback
  • Post-ride analysis and aerodynamic reports
  • Seamless integration with Garmin, Wahoo, Strava, and TrainingPeaks

Top 3 Benefits:

  • Save Watts: Reduce drag with optimal posture—gain more speed for the same effort.
  • Train Smarter: Know when and where your form breaks and fix it, session by session.
  • Ride with Confidence: Back your performance with hard data, not guesswork.

FAQs

What makes Airformatic different from other aero sensors like Notio or AeroPod?
Airformatic gives you real-time feedback while you ride—not just post-ride CdA data—so you can adjust your posture immediately and lock in better performance habits.
Does this work for amateurs, or just pros?
If you care about riding faster with less effort, Airformatic works for you. It's built for data-driven amateurs, triathletes, and performance-focused riders alike.
Will it interfere with my training or distract me?
You can choose from visual, haptic, or post-ride-only feedback. You stay in control of how you train.
What if I just want the hardware and not a subscription?
The base unit includes ride recording, posture logging, and basic insights. Subscriptions unlock real-time feedback, deeper analytics, and integration with other training tools.
Is it compatible with my current setup?
Yes, it works alongside Garmin, Wahoo, and most power meters. You don't need to change anything about your current training system.
Can I return it if it doesn't help me?
We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If it's not improving your training, we'll take it back—no questions asked.
